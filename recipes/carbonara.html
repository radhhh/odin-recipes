Carbonara Recipes

Description

Carbonara is very simple dish that originated from Italy. It has a creamy and salty flavor. The dish is only composed of 4 main ingredients :

  • Guanciale/Bacon
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Egg Yolk
  • Pasta

The steps for making the dish are :

  1. Cook the pasta until al dente, then drain while reserving the water
  2. At the same time, cook the bacon in a pan until it has rendered its fat
  3. Prepare a bowl of egg (1 egg per serving) and pecorino romano cheese, stir
  4. Remove the bacon from the pan
  5. Transfer the pasta into the pan
  6. Stir it consistently while adding the pasta water
  7. Put the stove into very low heat then add the egg mix
  8. Stir it continuously while still adding the pasta water until you reached the desired consistency
  9. Serve it while hot
